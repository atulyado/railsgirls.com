Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join this workshop?
This event welcomes all women — whether you're a student, a beginner, exploring a career change, or simply curious about tech. No prior coding knowledge is required. If you're eager to learn, you're invited!

What does it cost?
It's completely free! Just make sure to register in advance to save your spot. We'll also provide lunch and snacks during the day.

How many people can join?
Spots are limited, so we recommend registering early to secure your place.

Do I need coding experience?
No experience needed! The workshop is designed with beginners in mind. All you need is interest and enthusiasm for learning.

Will I get to code?
Yes! You'll get hands-on experience by building your own web app using Ruby on Rails. It's a great way to see what coding is really like.

Who will guide the sessions?
Experienced mentors and developers will lead the workshop. They're passionate about teaching and will be there to support you throughout the day.

What happens after I sign up?
You'll receive a confirmation email once you're registered. We'll also send you updates and details about the event, so keep an eye on your inbox!

Can men attend?
Yes — men can join if they're accompanying a female participant.

I already know how to code — how can I contribute?
Great! We're always looking for coaches. We'll host a short training session before the event to walk you through the material. Be sure to sign up as a coach!
